The Statute of ‘’Rixrank” social service, 23th of April 2015

Chapter I – General Provisions

Art. 1. Dictionary:
1) The Statute – The Statute of ‘’Rixrank” social service published on 23th of Apri 2015;
1) The Service – the website used to run ‘’Rixrank” social service;
2) The User – a natural person provided with full capacity to enter into legal transactions who is registered in The Service or a natural person provided with limited capacity to enter into legal transactions who reached the age of 16 and gained the statutory agent’s agreement and registered in The Service. The User possesses a personal account;
3) A Guest – an unregistered person with limited access to the Service; 4) An Administrator – a person who manages the Service; 5) The Managing Entity – the owners of the Service; 6) The Registration – a complex of actions which shall be carried out according to the instructions published in The Service. The Registration effects with creating an individual account;
7) A verification – clicking on the link in the message sent by the Managing Entity on the email address specified at registration;
8) An Account – a place in the Service where the User shall publish information and photographs or other contents;
9) A Nick – a User’s email address which is used as one of authentication tools to obtain an access to the Account.
10) A Password – an authentification tool needed to access the Accound in the form of user-created symbol combination.
11) Blocking - an option, thanks to which the User is able to prevent another User adding comments on his account and contact exchange.

Art. 2. 1. The Statute determines the terms of use of the Service. The Statute also defines the rights and obligations of the User, and the rights and duties and a liability of the Managing Entity.
2. Accepting the Statute is tantamount to a statement of hearing it and accepting its provisions.
Art. 3. Service name, logo, design, graphic design and its concept are protected by law.



Chapter II – Registration

Art. 4. Properly made Registration allows to create an Account. Having an Account allows full use of the Service.

Art. 5. Registration shall be deemed to have been made correctly, if they were made in accordance to the instructions on the Service.

Art. 6. Accepting the Statute is a condition of proper Registration.

Art. 7. Registration is deemed to be correct after verification of the Account.

Art. 8. A person making the Registration is obliged to provide their real data.

Art. 9. Registration to a third party without its consent and registration of fictitious accounts are prohibited.

Art. 10. The User can have only one Account.



Chapter III – Terms of use of the Service

Art. 10. 1. The User is obliged not to transfer the Password to third parties.
2. The User assumes the risk associated with the Password transfer to third parties.

Art. 11. The User agrees to use the data and photographs or other contents published on the Account for the operation, advertisement and marketing of the Service.

Art. 12. The User agrees to advertisement displays duriung the use of the Service.

Art. 13. The User agrees to the publication of their data and images in the rankings based on mutual marks of Users.

Art. 14. 1. The User can add comments to another User's Account, provided that this user did not previously blocked him.
2. The User can request for contact, provided that this User did not previously blocked him.


Art. 15. The User may not:
1) use phrases commonly regarded as vulgar or offensive, as well as in any way violate human dignity;
1) place pornographic materials;
2) promote other websites without consulting with Managing Entity;
3) spam;
4) place commercial content without the written consent of the Managing Entity;
5) be registered if he has been convicted of an offense of a sexual nature;

Art. 16. Failure to comply with the Statute, with particular emphasis on art. 13 may result in temporary or permanent blockage of an Account by the Administrator or the Managing Entity.

Art. 17. 1. The user may at any time request for the removal of the Account. For this purpose, the User shall be obliged to notify an Administrator or the Managing Entity.
2. The Managing Entity is obliged to remove the Account within 14 days from receiving by the Administrator or the Managing Entity a notification of the request to remove the Account from the User.

Art. 18. An Administrator or the Managing Entity may remove an Account not used for at least 6 months. “Not using” is tantamount to the absence of any User’s activity on his Account.



Chapter IV – Rights and obligations of the User

Art. 19. The User has the right to:
1) place data and photos, and other content on his Account;
1) edit data and photos, and other content on his Account;
2) remove data and photos, and other content from his Account;
3) notify the Administrator or the Managing Entity vulgar or offensive behavior towards him, as well as violating his dignity;
4) evaluate pictures of other Users in the way proposed by the Managing Entity;
5) add comments in the designated places;
6) request for a contact;
7) block other Users.

Art. 20. The User is obliged to :
1) inform the Administrator or the Managing Entity about any changes in his address of correspondence;
1) inform the Administrator or the Managing Entity about a suspection of existing an Account to a third party without its consent and registration of fictitious account;
2) inform the Administrator or the Managing Entity about a suspection of malfunction of the Service;
3) inform the Administrator or the Managing Entity about other Users’ violations of the provisions of Art. 15;
4) use the Service in terms of the present Statute.

Art. 21. The User takes full responsibility for all data and photographs, and other content uploaded into the Service.

Art. 22. The User, who accepts the Statute, states that he owes full rightsto data and images, and other content uploaded into the Service, as well as third-party’s consent to the publication in cases where third parties are present in the data and images, and other content.



Chapter V – Obligations of the Managing Entity

Art. 23. The Managing Entity is obliged to:
1) provide IT support service;
1) verify and where appropriate respond to information received from the User in accordance with Art. 20 points 1-4;
2) exercise due diligence to protect data and images, and other content placed by the User against interference by third parties, other than those to which he gave his Password;



Chapter VI – Liability of the Managing Entity

Art. 24. The Managing Entity shall not be liable in particular:
1) in case of damage incurred by the User due to hacker attack;
1) in case of damage incurred by the User due to phishing;
2) in case of damage incurred by the User as a result of the force majeure;
3) in case of damage incurred by the User as a result of other events related to the activities of the service, as long as the Managing Entity is not guilty of her existence;
4) In case of damage incurred by the User as a result of giving User’s Password to a third party;
5) for data and photographs and other content provided by Users.

Chapter VII – Recovering of the Password

Art. 25. In order to recover the password, the user is required to initiate the procedure by clicking "Forgot password" after a failed attempt to gain access to the profile. The password will be sent to the email address provided at registration.



Chapter VIII – Complaints and contact with the Managing Entity


Art. 26. Complaints should be directed to the email address contact@rixrank.com. That e-mail address is appropriate also in cases when it comes to contact with the Managing Entity.





Chapter IX – Final provisions


Art. 27. The User accepts the Service’s cookies policy.



Art. 28. The Statute is available in the current version at rixrank.com/statute.


Art. 29. The Managing Entity reserves the right to change the provisions of the Statute at any time, informing the Users in accordance with the requirements of regulations in this area. If the User does not accept amendments to the Statute, he has the right to terminate the agreement within 30 days from the date of change of the Statute by notifying he wants to remove profile (until then he is obliged by the Statute of existing content).
